## Curriculum (6 topics · 24 lessons · ~240 min)

### Topic 1 — How websites actually work (the 10-minute version) · 3 lessons · ~22 min
1. What happens when you type a URL (browsers, servers, files) — 8 min
2. Your toolkit: VS Code + a browser, set up in 5 minutes — 7 min
3. The project we're building (tour of the finished site) — 7 min

### Topic 2 — HTML that structures real pages, not toy examples · 5 lessons · ~48 min
4. Your first HTML file — skeleton, headings, paragraphs — 10 min
5. Links, images and buttons that do something — 10 min
6. Semantic structure: header, nav, main, section, footer — 10 min
7. Building the project's full page skeleton — 12 min
8. Forms: the contact form markup — 6 min

### Topic 3 — CSS layouts with Flexbox & Grid · 5 lessons · ~55 min
9. CSS fundamentals: selectors, the cascade, the box model — 12 min
10. Colors, backgrounds and units that make sense — 10 min
11. Flexbox: the navbar and hero layout — 12 min
12. Grid: the services card grid — 12 min
13. Positioning and z-index without the confusion — 9 min

### Topic 4 — Making it responsive — mobile, tablet, desktop · 4 lessons · ~42 min
14. Mobile-first thinking + the viewport meta tag — 8 min
15. Media queries: breakpoints that match real devices — 12 min
16. Fluid type and spacing with clamp() and rem — 10 min
17. Responsive images that don't wreck your layout — 12 min

### Topic 5 — Polish: fonts, spacing, and a pro-level finish · 4 lessons · ~40 min
18. Typography: choosing and loading fonts properly — 10 min
19. Whitespace and visual hierarchy (the 80/20 of good design) — 10 min
20. Hover states, transitions and micro-interactions — 10 min
21. Final QA pass: the checklist I run on client sites — 10 min

### Topic 6 — Publish your site live on the internet, free · 3 lessons · ~25 min
22. Free hosting options compared (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel) — 8 min
23. Deploying your site step by step — 10 min
24. What to learn next (+ join the Discord community) — 7 min

**Totals: 24 lessons · 232 min ≈ 4 h** ✔
